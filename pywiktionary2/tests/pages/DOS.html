[edit] English

[edit] Acronym

DOS

  1. (computing) Disk Operating System, sometimes MS-DOS for Microsoft-DOS, DR-DOS, etc.

[edit] Initialism

DOS

  1. (military) Date[s] of Service
  2. (military) Date of Separation
  3. (US) Department of State
  4. (sciences) Density of states
